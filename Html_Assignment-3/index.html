Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes.

Cooking Terms:

Bake:
To cook food by surrounding it with dry heat, usually in an oven.
Mix:
To combine two or more ingredients together until well blended.
